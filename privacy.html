Anime Deck

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 20, 2025  ·  Last updated: May 20, 2026

Anime Deck ("we", "our", or "the app") is an anime discovery and tier-list app for iOS, rated 17+ on the Apple App Store. This policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your choices. We are committed to keeping your data minimal and your experience transparent.

Short version: Anime Deck stores your ratings and preferences locally on your device only. We do not run servers, create accounts, or sell personal data. Ad-supported builds use Google AdMob, which may collect an advertising identifier if you allow tracking.

1. Information We Collect

Data stored locally on your device

  • Your anime ratings, tier lists, and watch history (SQLite database, never leaves your device).
  • Your taste preferences derived from ratings (a numeric vector used to personalise recommendations).
  • App settings such as theme and data-source preference.

Data fetched from third-party APIs (read-only)

  • Anime metadata (titles, cover images, genres, trailers) is fetched from the AniList public API. No personal data is sent to this service.

Data collected by third-party SDKs (see section 3 for details)

  • Google AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) and related ad-interaction signals if you grant tracking permission via the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt.
  • RevenueCat collects a pseudonymous device identifier to manage your in-app purchase and Pro subscription status.

2. How We Use Information

  • Recommendations — your local taste vector is used entirely on-device to rank and surface anime you might enjoy.
  • Advertising — ad-supported users may see personalised ads from Google AdMob. Personalisation is only enabled if you grant permission via the iOS ATT prompt. Declining does not affect app functionality; you will see non-personalised ads instead.
  • Subscriptions — RevenueCat is used solely to verify and restore your Pro subscription. We do not receive or store your payment details.

3. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Used to display banner and interstitial ads in the free tier. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad-interaction events. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

RevenueCat

Used to manage in-app purchases and the Pro subscription entitlement. RevenueCat stores a pseudonymous App User ID and purchase receipt data. Privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

AniList

Public anime catalog API used to populate the app's database. Only read requests are made; no user data is transmitted to this service. Privacy policy: anilist.co/terms

4. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS 14.5 and later, we display Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before enabling personalised advertising. If you tap Allow, AdMob may use your IDFA to show ads tailored to your interests. If you tap Ask App Not to Track, only non-personalised ads are shown and your IDFA is not used. You can change this decision at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data. Data is shared only with the third-party SDKs described in section 3, under their respective privacy policies, and only to the extent required for those services to function.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

All locally stored data (ratings, tier lists, preferences) is retained on your device until you delete the app. Deleting the app permanently removes all local data. Third-party SDKs (AdMob, RevenueCat) manage their own retention periods as described in their respective privacy policies.

7. Age Requirement & Children's Privacy

Anime Deck is rated 17+ on the Apple App Store. The app is intended for users aged 13 and older. It is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. Explicit adult content (anime flagged as adult by AniList) is hidden by default. If you believe a minor has provided personal information through a third-party SDK, please contact us (see section 10) and we will assist with removal.

8. Security

Because Anime Deck stores data locally and does not operate a server, there is no server-side database that could be breached. Your device's standard iOS security (data protection, sandboxing) applies to the local SQLite file. We recommend keeping your iOS version up to date.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
